About Us

"Formed in 1965, CSI is the first and largest body of computer professionals in India and has been instrumental in guiding the Indian IT industry down the right path. Across India, it has 72 chapters,500+ student branches, and more than 100000 members. Since the establishment of GRIET CSISB Student Chapter in the year 2014 it has been working to make it's students acquainted with the latest technology. This year 2018-19, CSI SB grew tremendously as over 160 students joined hands with CSI forming a chain of above 500+ student members."
